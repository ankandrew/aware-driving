FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I need to place my device?
Remember that the alerts emitted are based on the phone position. For example if you look completely down, an alert may be triggered because from the perspective of the camera you are “sleep” / don’t have your eyes open on the road. For best results, try to position at head level.
How can I disable certain alerts?
If you want to avoid certain alerts like i.e. “distracted” or “no face” ones, you can head to Settings and disable those. The only warning that can’t be disabled is the “asleep” one.
What is Intelligent Distracted?
With this option Aware Driving will predict whether your vehicle is moving and if it still doesn't emit “distracted” alerts (No need to pay attention while you are not driving)
What is the difference between “Test” and “Start” mode?
The idea is that you try the app first in “Test” mode and once you are comfortable with the alerts and sounds, you can go with “Start” mode to let the app run in the background while you use other apps like i.e. Maps.
How does Driver Score work?
