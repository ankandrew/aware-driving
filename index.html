FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Test and Start mode?
The app works the same in both, but in Test you see the camera preview for adjustment and in Start mode you can keep the app running in the background.
Where do I need to place my device?
Alerts emitted are based on the device position. If you look completely down, an alert may be triggered because from the perspective of the camera you are Asleep (also means you don't have your eyes open on the road). Try to position it at head level.
Alerts stop in the background
Don't enable "battery optimization" for this app, it's likely that Android will kill the app while running in the background. Also, if you open an app that uses the camera, it may interfere with Aware Driving while it's running in the background.
What is Intelligent Distracted?
Aware Driving will predict whether your vehicle is moving and if it's still it won't emit Distracted alerts (there is no need to pay attention while you are not driving). This is an experimental feature.
Where are Images analyzed?
All the image analysis is done offline on your device. No image ever leaves your device, so no internet connection is ever required.
