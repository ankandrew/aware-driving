Aware Driving

Driving Assistance

Aware Driving (AD) is a mobile app that will assist you while you are driving. Use it with your regular smartphone, there’s no need to buy extra equipment.

Privacy

Images are analyzed on the device and never leave your smartphone

Offline

No need to have WiFi/GPS enabled for it to work

Availability

No need to buy any equipment, it’s available on your device

Free

It’s completely free to use

Alerts

When both of your eyes are completely closed for a given amount of time, you'll be alerted!

When you look sideways for a given amount of time you'll be also alerted!

And when your face can't be seen for a given amount of time you'll get also alerted!
